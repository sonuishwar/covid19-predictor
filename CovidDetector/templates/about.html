COVID-19 Probability Detector : Solution to this Pandemic

Many people to test in too little time with too little resources ?
Here's the solution.This application developed by us uses Machine Learning algorithms to predict the probability
of Covid-19 infection in a person. The probability is calculated after examining the symptoms of the patient.
This gives the advantage of classifying people based on the likeliness of having Covid-19 and test the people with
higher probability at the earliest. The people with lower risk can quarantine themselves till their chance arrives.
This will filter out people who genuinely need medical attention from the masses and reduce the burden on the medical
service providers of India. This application has been developed by us to deal with extraordinary situation like Covid-19.
